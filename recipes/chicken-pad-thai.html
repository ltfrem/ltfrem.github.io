Chicken Pad Thai

  • 2 tbsp curry powder
  • ⅓ cup peanut butter
  • 1 13.5oz can coconut milk
  • 3 tbsp fish sauce
  • 2 tbsp lime juice
  • 2 tbsp honey
  • 1 tbsp chili sauce
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp rice vinegar
  • 1 tbsp minced garlic (3-4 cloves)
  • Minced peanuts & green onions for garnish
  • 1 package brown rice pad thai noodles
  • 2 large chicken breasts
  1. Mix all ingredients except for noodles in a sauce pan and cook on medium, stirring occasionally.
  2. Cook noodles according to package directions.
  3. Add sauce and cooked chicken to noodles, top with peanuts & onions.
