Zucchini Herb Casserole

  • ⅔ cup uncooked long grain white rice
  • ⅔ cup water
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • 1 ½ lbs. zucchini, cubed
  • 1 cup sliced green onions (or yellow onion)
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • ½ teaspoon basil
  • ½ teaspoon paprika
  • ½ teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 ½ cups seeded, chopped tomatoes
  • 1 cups shredded cheddar cheese, divided
  1. Combine the rice and water in a saucepan, and bring it to a boil. Reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer until rice is tender.
  2. Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Lightly grease a shallow 1 ½ quart casserole dish.
  3. Heat the oil in a skillet over medium heat and cook the zucchini, green onion, and garlic 5 minutes, or until tender.
  4. Add basil, paprika, and oregano.
  5. Mix in the cooked rice, tomatoes, and half the cheese.
  6. Continue to cook and stir until heated through.
  7. Transfer to the prepared casserole dish and top with remaining cheese.
  8. Base uncovered 20 minutes ot until cheese is melted and bubbly.
