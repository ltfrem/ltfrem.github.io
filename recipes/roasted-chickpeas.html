Crispy Parmesan Roasted Chickpeas

Makes 3 cups

  • 2 ounces parmesan cheese (not pre-grated)
  • 2 15oz. cans chickpeas or 3 cups cooked
  • Olive oil, salt, pepper
  1. Preheat oven to 425.
  2. Pour 2 tablespoons olive oil into 4 or 5-quart baking dish. Place in preheated oven.
  3. In a medium bowl, combine the chickpeas, 2 teaspoons olive oil, parmesan cheese, ½ teaspoon salt, and ⅛ teaspoon pepper. Mix well until chickpeas are thoroughly coated.
  4. Carefully remove the hot baking dish from the oven and pour the chickpea mixture into it.
  5. Use a wooden spoon to spread the chickpeas into a single layer. Roast for 10 minutes, then stir with the wooden spoon and continue to roast until crispy and golden brown, an additional 7-9 minutes. Season with additional salt and pepper as desired. Serve hot or at room temperature. (Do not drain chickpeas on paper towels or they will lose their crispiness).
