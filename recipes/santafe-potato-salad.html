Santa Fe Potato Salad

Makes 6 servings

  • ½ cup olive oil
  • 6 tablespoons fresh lime juice
  • 3 large garlic cloves, peeled
  • 2 tablespoons chopped jalapeño chilies with seeds
  • 3½ teaspoons ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 15ox can golden hominy, drained
  • ¾ cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • ⅔ cup peeled, diced jicama
  • ½ cup chopped white onion
  • 2 lbs medium-large yukon gold potatoes, unpeeled (about 6 potatoes)
  1. Pureé olive oil, lime juice, garlic, jalapeños, cumin, oregano in blender until almost smooth. Season generously with salt.
  2. Pour dressing into medium bowl. Mix in hominy, ½ cup cilantro, jicama, and onion. Let stand for 30 minutes.
  3. Steam potatoes until tender, about 30 minutes. Let cool 15 minutes, then peel. Cut potatoes lengthwise in half, then crosswise into ½-inch thick slices. Place in large bowl.
  4. Mix potatoes with hominy mixture. Season to taste with salt and pepper. Sprinkly with ¼ cup cilantro.
  5. Note: can be prepared up to 2 hours ahead. Cover and let stand at room temperature.
