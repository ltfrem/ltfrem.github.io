Vegetable-Fried Farro

Servings: 1 loaf

  • 2 ripe bananas
  • 2 cups rolled oats (or 1 cup rolled oats + 1 cup flour)
  • ⅓ cup coconut oil, melted
  • 2 eggs
  • ½ cup honey or maple syrup
  • ½ cup milk
  • ½ teaspoon turmeric
  • ½ teaspoon cinnamon
  • ½ teaspoon sea salt
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • ½ teaspoon baking powder
  • ⅓ cup walnuts, chopped
  • ⅓ cup mini chocolate chips
  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees.
  2. Process oats in a blender until they resemble a fine flour. Add oat flour to a large mixing bowl along with the rest of the dry ingredients.
  3. In a separate bowl, combine the bananas, coconut oil, honey/syrup, eggs, and milk. Whisk together and gradually stir into the dry ingredients.
  4. Fold in the walnuts and chocolate chips.
  5. Bake for 50 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted comes out clean.
