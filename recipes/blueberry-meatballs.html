Blueberry Breakfast Meatballs

From https://shop.feedingthefrasers.com/blogs/recipes-1/blueberrybreakfastmeatball

  • 1 large sweet potato, peeled
  • 2 lbs ground pork
  • 1 ½ cup finely chopped kale
  • 1 tbsp maple syrup
  • 1 ½ tsp salt
  • 1 ½ tsp fresh chopped thyme(or ¾ tsp dried)
  • 1 ½ tsp fresh chopped sage (or ¾ tsp dried)
  • ⅔ cup blueberries (almost 1 whole package)
  1. Preheat oven to 400. Line rimmed baking sheet with parchment paper and set aside.
  2. Grate sweet potato until there is 1 ½ cups.
  3. In a stand mixer, add sweet potato, kale, pork, maple syrup, salt, thyme, and sage. Mix until fully combined. Lastly, add blueberries and mix carefully taking care not to smoosh them.
  4. Using a cookie scoop, shape each scoop into a ball and place on lined baking sheet.
  5. Bake for 20 minutes until golden. Let cool 5 minutes before serving.
