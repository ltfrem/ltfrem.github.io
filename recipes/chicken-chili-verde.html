Slow Cooker Chicken Chili Verde

  • 1 lb. boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 1 lb. boneless skinless chicken thighs
  • ¾ tomatillos, husked and quartered
  • 2 poblano peppers, seeded and chopped (use bell pepper for less heat)
  • 1 jalapeño pepper, seeded and chopped
  • ½ onion, sliced
  • ⅓ cup cilantro, chopped
  • 1 garlic clove, minced
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 2 teaspoons cumin
  • salt and pepper
  1. Place chicken in slow cooker and top with ingredients
  2. Cook for 4 hours or until chicken easily shreds with a fork.
