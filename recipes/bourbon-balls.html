Bourbon Balls (great grandma's recipe)

Makes 20-22 balls

  • 1 6oz. package semi-sweet chocolate chips
  • 3 tbsp white corn syrup
  • ½ cup bourbon
  • 2½ cups crushed vanilla wafers
  • ½ cup powdered sugar
  • 1 cup walnuts, chopped
  1. Melt chocolate chips. Remove from heat, and add syrup and bourbon.
  2. Combine crushed wafers, powdered sugar, and nuts. Add to chocolate mixture and mix well. Let stand 30 minutes.
  3. Form into 1-inch balls, then roll in powdered sugar.

Can be kept in a tightly covered container for 3-4 weeks.
