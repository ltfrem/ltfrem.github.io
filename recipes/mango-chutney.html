Mango Chutney

Makes 5 6oz. jars

  • 1 ¼ cups granulated sugar
  • 1 cup white vinegar
  • 4 cups chopped peeled ripe mangoes (½-inch pieces...3-4 mangoes)
  • 1 cup finely chopped yellow onion
  • ½ cup finely chopped red bell pepper
  • ½ cup golden raisins
  • 1 tablespon grated fresh ginger
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • ½ teaspoon yellow mustard seeds
  • ¼ teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes
  • salt
  1. In a large pot, combine the sugar and white viengar and bring to a boil. Cook, stirring, until the sugar has dissolved, about 3 minutes. Add the mangoes, onion, bell pepper, raisins, ginger, garlic, mustard seeds, red pepper flakes. and ½ teaspoon salt and stir to combine. Reduce the heat to low and simmer, stirring occasionally, until thickened and syrupy, 45-55 minutes. Remove from heat and let cool. Pour into 6oz. glass jars and store in the refrigerator for up to 4 months.
