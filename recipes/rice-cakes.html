Bacon Maple Almond Butter fresh rice cakes (running snack)

Cook time: 30 minutes

  • 3 cups uncooked calrose or other medium-grain 'sticky' rice
  • 4 cups water
  • 8oz cooked bacon
  • 1 cup warmed almond butter
  • Liquid amino acid, to taste
  • 1¼ cup rice vinegar
  • Maple syrup, to taste/li>
  • Salt, to taste
  1. Cook the rice according to package directions.
  2. While rice is cooking, chop up the bacon and fry in a medium sauté pan. When crispy, drain off fat and soak up excess fat with paper towels.
  3. Mix wet ingredients (warmed almond butter, liquid aminos, rice vinegar, maple syrup) in a bowl. Mix into rice while it is still hot.
  4. Fold in bacon.
  5. Cut and wrap individual cakes.
