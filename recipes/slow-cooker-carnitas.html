Slow-cooker Carnitas

Makes 16 servings

  • 4-5 lb pork shoulder
  • 1 onion, finely sliced
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp garilc powder
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp oregano
  • Juice of 2 limes (or 2 tbsp apple cider vinegar)
  • 2 tbsp maple syrup (optional)
  1. Place onions in bottom of slow cooker.
  2. Mix spices and salt. Rub over pork shoulder and place in the slow cooker.
  3. Add lime juice or vinegar.
  4. Cook on high for 6-8 hours. When finished, the pork should easily shred with a fork.
  5. Remove pork and either eat as is or with tacos.
  6. Note: this recipe freezes extremely well.
