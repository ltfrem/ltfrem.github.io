Coconut Chicken Curry

Makes 8 servings

  • 1 medium onion, finely diced
  • 1 cup fresh green beans, trimmed and cut into 2-inch pieces
  • 1 green bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 red bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp freshly grated ginger
  • 1 tbsp coconut oil
  • 2 tbsp curry powder or garam masala
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 15 oz can coconut milk
  • 16 oz chicken bone broth
  • 16 oz cooked, shredded chicken
  • 3-4 cups fresh spinach
  • Juice of 2 limes
    • Dash of soy sauce to taste
  • Red pepper flakes (optional)
  1. In a medium pot over medium-high heat, sauté onion in coconut oil until lightly browned.
  2. Add all remaining vegetables, garlic, ginger, curry powder, and salt. Cook for 5 minutes.
  3. Add coconut milk, broth, and chicken.
  4. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  5. Add lime and soy sauce to taste.
  6. Just before serving, add fresh spinach and stir to wilt.
  7. Note: this recipe freezes extremely well.
