Coffee Liqueur (great grandma's recipe)

  • 4 cups water
  • 3 cups sugar
  • 10 tbsp instant coffee
  • 5 tbsp pure vanilla
  • One fifth cheap vodka
  1. Using a big pan, boil water, sugar, and coffee slowly for 1 hour, watching for the last 30 minutes.
  2. When mixture boils up remove from stove, and let cool and skim.
  3. Pour mixture into ½ gallon wine bottle and add vodka and vanilla.
