Pineapple Chicken Enchiladas

Makes approximately 6-8 servings. Serve with Rice-A-Roni spanish rice.

  • 1 lb chicken breast, diced
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 20oz. can pineapple chunks or tidbits
  • 1 4oz. can green chilies
  • 2 cups shredded mexican cheese
  • 1 10oz. can enchilada sauce
  • 8 whole wheat tortillas
  • Cilantro for topping
  1. Preheat oven to 350.
  2. Cook chicken, onion, bell pepper.
  3. Add green chilies, pineapple.
  4. Put mixture into tortillas, adding a small handful of cheese and a pinch of cilantro to each.
  5. Heat up enchilada sauce and the remaining pineapple juice from can.
  6. Pour mixture over everything; add remaining cheese.
  7. Spray tin foil with nonstick spray and cover. Bake 40-45 minutes (high altitude).
  8. Remove tin foil and cook another 5-10 minutes until cheese is melted and sauce near edges is bubbling.
  9. Top with more fresh cilantro and serve.
