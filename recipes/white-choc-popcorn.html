White Chocolate Popcorn

A favorite from my childhood.

  • 8 cups cooked plain popcorn
  • 11 ounces white chocolate chips
  • 10-12 ounces M&M's
  • Sea salt
  1. If you have a food processor, start by taking half the M&M's and pulsing them so that you're left with pieces of M&M's.
  2. Melt the chocolate according to package directions. Place half the popcorn in a large bowl. Pour half the melted chocolate over the popcorn. Sprinkle with half the M&M's and desired amount of sea salt. Use a large spoon to combine all the ingredients.
  3. Line two rimmed baking sheets with wax paper and pour the popcorn on the tray so that the white chocolate can set.
  4. Place the remaining popcorn in the large bowl, add in the remaining chocolate, the rest of the M&M's, and sea salt. Stir to combine.
  5. Pour the mixture onto a rimmed baking sheet lined with wax paper and give it about 10-15 minutes for the chocolate to set.
  6. Store in an airtight container.
