Irish Cream Liqueur (great grandma's recipe)

  • 1 can sweetened condensed milk
  • 8 oz whiskey
  • 4 whole eggs
  • 1 tsp chocolate syrup
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  1. Blend whiskey, eggs, chocolate syrup and vanilla.
  2. Add sweetened condensed milk and refrigerate overnight.
  3. Shake before pouring; keep in refrigerator.


grandma's note: 2 shots irish cream liqueur + 1 shot amaretto (blow job)
