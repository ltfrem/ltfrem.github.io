Chicken & Vegetable Soup

Makes 4 servings.

  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 3 large carrots, peeled and chopped
  • 4 stalks celery, chopped
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 tsp salt
  • ½ tsp pepper
  • ½ tsp dried thyme
  • 6 cups chicken bone broth
  • 1 lb chicken meat, cooked and chopped
  • ½ cup heavy cream
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 2 tbsp fresh parsley (optional)
  1. Cook chicken if uncooked.
  2. In a large pot over high heat, sauté all vegetables in butter with salt, pepper, and thyme until lightly browned and fragrant.
  3. Add chicken bone broth and bring to a boil.
  4. Add chicken, heavy cream, and lemon juice. Reduce heat and simmer for 5 minutes. Adjust seasonings to taste. Serve with fresh parsley.
