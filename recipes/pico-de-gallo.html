Pico de Gallo

Makes 2 ½ cups

  • 1 ¼ pounds ripe tomatoes, chopped
  • ½ cup finely chopped red onion
  • 2 teaspoons chopped fresh cilantro (optional)
  • 1 teaspoon finely chopped jalapeño pepper, or to taste
  • 1 tablespoon fresh lime juice
  • ¾ teaspoon sea salt
  • ⅛ teaspoon black pepper
  1. In a medium bowl, stir together all the ingredients. Store in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 1 week.
