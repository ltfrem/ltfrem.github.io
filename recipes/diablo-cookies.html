Diablo Cookies

  • 1 ¾ cups flour
  • 1 cup cocoa powder
  • 2 tsp baking soda
  • ½ - 1 tsp cayenne pepper
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 1 ½ cups chocolate chips
  • 2 eggs
  • ¾ cup brown sugar
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 3 tbsp grated fresh ginger, or ginger juice
  • 1 tbsp vanilla
  • 1 tsp flaky salt
  1. Preheat oven to 375.
  2. Sift flour, cocoa powder, baking soda, cayenne, cinnamon in large bowl.
  3. Cream butter & sugars. Add eggs, ginger, and vanilla.
  4. Mix dry ingredients into wet until combined. Stir in chocolate chips.
  5. With ¼ cup measure, scoop dough and slightly flatten with bottom of cup. Sprinkle with flaky salt.
  6. Bake at 375, 11-13 minutes until cookies start to crack. Remove from oven, cook on baking sheet 3 minutes before moving to cooling rack.
