Buffalo Twice-Baked Potatoes (adapted from Thug Kitchen)

  • 6 small Russet potatoes
  • 2 tbsp high-heat oil (sunflower, avocado)

  • Buffalo Sauce
  • 1 cup Frank's Redhot sauce
  • ¼ cup apple cider vinegar or white vinegar
  • 1 tsp Bragg's Aminos or soy sauce
  • 4 tbsp non-dairy butter or refined coconut oil
  • 1 tsp garlic powder

  • Filling
  • 1 cup unsweetened non-dairy milk
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 medium yellow onion, chopped
  • 1 tbsp Bragg's Aminos or soy sauce
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • ½ cup nutritional yeast
  • ¼ cup chopped green onions
  • Optional toppings: chopped chives, chopped celery
  1. Place the potatoes on a baking sheet. Rub them with some oil and bake for 1 hour until you can stick a knife through them without any resistance.
  2. Throw the hot sauce, vinegar, soy sauce, butter, and garlic powder into a saucepan over medium heat. Let it simmer, stirring occasionally, until all the butter has melted, about 5 minutes.
