Poblano Tahini Sauce

Makes 1 cup

  • 1 ½ cups roughly chopped poblano peppers
  • 1 clove garlic, quartered
  • ⅓ cup tahini
  • 3 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • ½ teaspoon sea salt
  • ⅛ teaspoon black pepper
  1. Combine all the ingredients in a blender or food processor, add ¼ cup water, and blend until smooth. If the mixture is too thick, add additional water as needed to reach desired consistency.
  2. Store in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 1 week.
