Low-carb Shepherd's Pie

Makes 6-8 servings

  • Filling:
  • 1 lb grass fed ground beef
  • 3 oz grass fed beef liver, finely chopped (optional)
  • 1 small onion, finely diced
  • 3 carrots, peeled and finely diced
  • 2 stalks celery, finely diced
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 1 tsp salt
  • ½ tsp pepper
  • 2 tsp dried thyme
  • Cauliflower topping:
  • 1 large head cauliflower, chopped
  • 4 tbsp butter
  • 1 tsp salt
  • ½ tsp pepper
  1. Preheat oven to 400.
  2. Steam cauliflower while preparing the filling, which takes 10-15 minutes depending on the size of cauliflower pieces.
  3. In a large skillet, cook ground beef over medium-high heat. If the pan is dry, add a bit of butter or coconut oil.
  4. With a spatula, break up meat into bite-sized pieces. Once browned, add liver and cook for 1-2 minutes.
  5. Remove from heat and place in a 9x13 baking dish. Do not drain fat.
  6. In the same pan, add butter onion, carrots, celery, garlic, salt, pepper, and thyme.
  7. Cook for 10 minutes, being sure to scrape up browned bits.
  8. Mash steamed cauliflower wth butter, salt, and pepper. spread over top of meat and vegetables.
  9. Bake for 20 minutes, until cauliflower is lightly browned.
