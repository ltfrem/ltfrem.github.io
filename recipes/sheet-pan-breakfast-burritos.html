Sheet Pan Breakfast Burritos

Makes 10 burritos.

  • 2 bell peppers, diced
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2-3 large potatoes, diced
  • 16 oz. turkey sausage
  • 450g (2.75 cups) egg whites
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 cup cottage cheese
  • 1 tbsp corn starch
  • 1 cup green chilies, diced
  • 10 burrito size tortillas
  • 2 cups plain greek yogurt
  • 3 tbsp hot sauce
  • 1 cup shredded cheese
  1. Put bell peppers, onion, potatoes on a baking sheet. Drizzle with olive oil, salt, pepper and bake at 450 for 25 minutes.
  2. Remove from oven. Break apart sausage over the top of cooked veggies and potatoes, sprinkle with spices (garlic, salt, pepper, cumin, etc.) and cook for 5 minutes.
  3. Mix together egg whites, eggs, cottage cheese, corn starch, and green chilies.
  4. Reduce heat to 400. Pour egg mixture over sausage and veggies and top with cheese. Bake 25-30 minutes (until middle is firm).
  5. Mix greek yogurt and hot sauce together and spread equal amounts over 10 tortillas.
  6. Remove everything from oven and cut into 10 equally-sized pieces. Place in tortillas.
