Beef and Quinoa Enchilada Bake

Servings: 6

  • ½ cup quinoa
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 small onion (about ⅓ cup) finely chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves minced
  • 1 10oz. can enchilada sauce
  • 1 14oz. can petite diced tomatoes
  • 1 cup black beans, rinsed and drained
  • 1 8.75oz. can yellow whole kernel corn
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • ½ teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • ½ ground black pepper
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar jack cheese
  • 1-2 tablespoons fresh cilantro, finely chopped for garnish
  • chopped tomatoes for garnish
  1. Fill a small pot with 1 cup water and cook quinoa according to package directions. Set aside.
  2. Heat olive oil in a large nonstick skillet, over medium heat. Add ground beef and onions and cook for 8-10 minutes crumbling beef until no longer pink. Drain fat and return to burner.
  3. Add quinoa, garlic, enchilada suace, tomatoes, black beans, corn, cumin, chili powder, paprika, salt and pepper until well combined. Cook over medium-low heat until heated through, about 15 minutes.
  4. Sprinkle with cheese. Cover and cook until cheese melts, about 2-3 minutes.
  5. Sprinkle with chopped cilantro and chopped tomatoes and serve.
